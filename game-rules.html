Guess My Number Rules :

  1. » Player Starts with 20 points You have to guess the Mysterious number before your score points run out.

  2. » Each time you guess the number, you lose one point.

  3. » When you reach zero points. It's game over, you lose and have to try again by Clicking Again button.

  4. » Each time you guess the correct number , your high score will be updated by comparing previous high score with the new acquired high score.

Ocean Dicee Rules :

  1. » This is a Two Player Game.

  2. » Player 1, starts game and rolls the dice. they can click the hold button to store the points they collected or keep on rolling.

  3. » when dice 1 is rolled , game switches to the next player and all un-stored points collected by the previous player will be lost.

  4. » The first player who collects 50 points be the winner of the match.

  5. » Once match is over, you can click at the start New Game button to play again.

Rock Paper Scissors Rules :

  1. » Scissors cuts Paper (✌️ > ✋)

  2. » Paper covers Rock (✋ > ✊)

  3. » Scissors Cuts Paper (✊ > ✌️)

  4. » Player With the most points wins the game.

  5. » Once the match is over, you can click at the New Game button to play again.
