Guess My Number Rules :

  1. » Player Starts with 30 score points and you have to guess the Mysterious number before your points run out.

  2. » Each guess must be between 1 and 30 .

  3. » Each time you guess the correct number, your high score will be updated.📝

  4. » A high score of 30 completes game 🥲.

  5. » Each time you guess the number, you lose one point.

  6. » When you reach zero points. It's game over, you lose and have to try again.

Pig Dicee Rules :

  1. » This is a Two Player Game.

  2. » Player 1, starts the game and rolls the dice. You may click the hold button to store the points you have collected or keep on rolling at your own risk. 🫡

  3. » when dice 1 is rolled , game switches to the next player and all un-stored points collected by the previous player will be lost.🫠

  4. » The first player who collects 50 or more points is the winner of the match.🔥

  5. » Once the match is over, you can click at the New Game button to play again.

Rock Paper Scissors Rules :

  1. » Scissors cuts Paper (✌️ > ✋)

  2. » Paper covers Rock (✋ > ✊)

  3. » Scissors Cuts Paper (✊ > ✌️)

  4. » Player With the most points wins the game.

  5. » Once the match is over, you can click at the New Game button to play again.

  6. » 🍏Green Glow means : User wins round.

  7. » 🍎Red Glow means : User lose round.

  8. » 🍊Orange Glow means : It's a draw.
